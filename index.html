About me

My profile

The Multimedia Design education has really opened my eyes for even more creative ways to express yourself. I'm looking forward to contribute with my ideas and the skills I have learned but also to learn even more. Graphical design definitely catches my interest and I would love to evovle myself with making graphical content to various channels. In a team am I helpful, positive and ambitious. The coolest about this business is that you will never finish learning and that's definitely something I value. My first language is Danish but I speak fluent English and some german.

My personally

I live in a small city named Fensmark right outside Næstved. Here I live with my boyfriend and our two lovely dogs. I have many different interests and I love to learn new things. I love animals and some day I dream about having my own small farm and in that connection I have started to learn ride. In the summer I enjoy a nice ride on my motorcycle and when the weather isn't for that I craft various things in my workshop. Besides that I play some videogames and my boyfriend and I enjoy developing small code projects and apps in our freetime.
